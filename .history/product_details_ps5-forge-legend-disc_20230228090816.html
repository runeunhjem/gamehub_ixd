Product Description

Forge Legend is an upcoming action role-playing game published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The initial release date for Forge Legend ps5 was 25 September 2022. It will feature an immersive single-player story of beauty, magic and survival in 4K HDR @ 60fps.

The game sets the player in an open-world game with the scenic Great Highlands of Galander all around them. Taking control over a huntress, the player has to survive in a world full of dangerous magic and savage beasts ready to attack at any moment.

Plot

The game tells the story of Alaidarel, a member of the Galden tribe, as she becomes a leader of an expedition band to find the source of the mysterious plague in the arcane frontier known as the Great Highlands of Galander. During the journey through those uncharted lands, she discovers new regions destroyed by storms and scary beasts. She encounters a tribe of nomadic raiders who tamed the scary beasts and now use them as weapons.

Gameplay

Forge Legend is an action RPG in which the player controls Alaidarel, a huntress wishing to survive in this dangerous world fraught with danger. The exploration of the Great Highlands of Galander makes the journey mysterious, dangerous and full of adventure.

The player will explore breathtaking locations and different regions with various biomes. Exciting features include underwater exploration.

Such aspects of the game as melee combat, climbing mechanics, freeform climbing and tools such as Shieldsword are so easy to use. You feel comfortable controlling the character.

Additionally, quests support variety in missions with worthwhile rewards.

Key Features

The game features a mesmerising single-player campaign with the story of Alaidarel, a young huntress from the Galden tribe, in the spotlight. Explore the Great Highlands of Galander, crawling with enemies and full of secrets to uncover.
